Dear Fine Gentlemen,


And for all the men, this post isn't exactly a man's-guide on how to beat feminism, it's more of a relational post from one man to another, sharing the insides of a man's life in 21st century and humbly saying, you aren't alone. I'm with you. So are the others.

This post isn't exactly an anti-feminist frustration post on how all of us men wish there had been no feminism, but it's more like me asking all of you fine men to calm down and telling you everything is fine.

Feminism is good. Especially for women. And of course, why not. Women are the ones who have always sort of lived under a diktat. Told to wear, told to keep quiet, told to talk, told to dress up, told to dress down, told to be brave, told to be complacent, told to live, told to die on the piers on dead husbands, told to stay away from school, told to be the sole bread earner, told to leave, told to stay….the list goes on. Has anything been their choice?

The subtleness of the abuse has got a lot of us angry. Especially, women. They required feminism. And so did we.

But I also admit amidst this hullabaloo of feminism, there had been a rise against anti-masculinism. I don't want to spark another debate on how our justice system has horrendously given full autonomy and unjust powers to women of our country and outside too. We already know that. I understand. All us men do.

For example, if the death of the woman is caused by burns or bodily injury within 7 years of marriage, it's the husband's fault. This has been stated in Section 304 B of the Indian Penal Code. No matter what the actual reason of the accident might be.

Or, let's say, According to Section 497 of the Indian Penal Code , if the husband commits adultery with the wife of another man, he can be prosecuted for the same. But if a woman commits adultery with the husband of another woman, she cannot be prosecuted.

Also, we know if we spoke out for it, no one would listen to us and just simply tag us with one of those feminazi insults. Because, hell we are men. And empowerment is only meant for women in this country. So, I'm just asking you to calm down and bring a smile on your face. That's it. I’m proud of being a man and I’m pretty sure, so are you.

You all perfectly understand the default "insult", routinely deployed towards any guy who even remotely questions feminist ideology or any special treatment of women is the tag of a "misogynist." You can be a misogynist for most things these days, from pointing out the gender pay gap isn’t real to saying men’s football is better than women’s. Note: women can’t be misogynists (nor sexist), just as black people can’t be racist. It’s a boys-only club.

See. I understand. All us men do. You are not alone.

Or for the sake of the fun, let's say, a cisgendered man is a man who isn’t transgender, meaning the vast majority of mankind. This means you cannot possibly understand the plight of the other 50 genders out there, from genderqueers and pangenders to fully-formed transgenders. To be “cis-white” also means you can’t understand racism. You’re the lowest of the low.

A ‘f**kboy’ is a young man who sleeps with women without any intention of having a relationship with them. Which is a lot of men. They’re dismissed as Tinder-driven, callous womanizers, emotional vampires and losers, even though lots of women have sex with them – which is what really infuriates the feminists. But obviously, women are the most endangered form of species on the planet, so no such insulting words for them.

Do you all remember when about an year ago, the infamous Priyanka Chopra exclaimed on international media, "I don't need men other than for children." Or when Deepika Padukone released that bad-ass feminist video wherein she said, "To sleep or not to sleep out of marriage is my own choice."

Imagine a man saying that. Period.

I am not going against women for categorizing all men as rapists or ass-holes. But I also know, no one would listen to us even if we begged them not to do it. Because, we don't exactly have an empowerment movement for us.

It’s okay if a woman wanted to show up A little bit of skin and that indeed is no sin. But what when that little equals objectification over sexualisation which causes one hell of a sensation. I mean enough with the overgeneralization.

I say, men and women can never be equal. We were not meant to be equal. Men are better than women in certain things. Just like women are better than men at certain other tasks.

The thing is how do we explain the simple fact that men are indeed to these feminist women who have already been brainwashed. Yes, some women are stronger than some men. But why does everything have to be generic ? Men are physically stronger in most times. It's proven by biology. And you can't fight biology.

It's exactly like women are better multitaskers than men because their brains have been programmed to. And ours aren't.And the point is, we aren't ashamed of admitting this. In fact, we are totally cool with it.

Also, if you ever feel totally useless about getting friend-zoned or bro-zoned from a woman, don't worry. Just remember 500 years ago, the Australian army attacked itself and lost 10,000 men. You are not useless yet, mate !

And also, just for the sake of it, once the Roman Emperor Caligula waged a war against Poseidon. And he had his army walk to the beach and start stabbing the water. YOU ARE NOT USELESS, MATE!

I know, we hear about such useless debates all around the world day by day and we can't speak because hell, we are men and who would listen to us. See. I understand all of it. And I'm pretty sure, you do too. So, just smile and calm down. Because we are men. One more thing we are better than women at is not complaining and shouting. And I'm proud of it.

Some will even promise to stay with you in all times of distress until the point you promise to be her knight in shining armour. But take my advice and never marry a girl until you are financially stable and ready to buy her a Castle in France. Like, hell yeah, she has promised to stay with you if god forbid any unforeseen circumstances strike you in the future. But even if she earns a lot of wealth herself as an independent and emotionally strong woman, never assume that wealth to be yours. But always let her have your wealth (Only after you are comfortable with her using it to buy some puppies.) But anyway, I really don't see the reason to deliberately drag someone else's daughter into poverty because she made a mere promise.

Because, I'm pretty sure, no matter how much your own daughter's future husband might love her, you wouldn't want to her to go from a lavish life straight to poverty.

But my main motive of this post is to urge all of you to not get frustrated by such anti-masculinist feminism, that you just stop being fine gentlemen and lose your subtle manners and classic etiquettes and your utmost respect for women. Because, I have seen a lot of men doing that lately. Never ever lose your respect for women. Go ahead, flirt with her. Make her dazzle. Do it until she bites her lips. But before you cross the line and go overboard, remember you are talking to someone else’s daughter. And maintain the goddamn eye contact with her.

Because I do have a feeling that women won't understand all this if we tried to explain. So, logically it's a waste of energy for us men.

We are men. We have been doing this for centuries. Be subtle. Be discreet. Be the prince of her princess. Be the gangsta and get the job done. But without making any noise.
